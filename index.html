translationRecorder

What is it?

translationRecorder is an open source solution to oral Bible translation. Created as a part of the Five Weeks of Code project, it provides a simple way for a someone who has never used recording software to quickly begin translating the Bible into their heart language.

How much is it?

Because every part of translationRecorder is open source, you can use it completely free of charge and anything you make with it can be distributed without asking permission from anyone.

Where to get it?

translationRecorder is not currently on the GooglePlay Store, but you can download the APK for Android devices here.
